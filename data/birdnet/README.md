# Bird Taxonomy Data

`ebird_clements_taxonomy_v2025.csv` is a compact lookup generated from the Cornell Lab of Ornithology eBird/Clements Checklist v2025 integrated CSV. The station uses it to map BirdNET common/scientific names and genera to family and order.

Suggested source citation: Clements, J. F., P. C. Rasmussen, T. S. Schulenberg, M. J. Iliff, J. A. Gerbracht, D. Lepage, A. Spencer, S. M. Billerman, B. L. Sullivan, M. Smith, and C. L. Wood. 2025. The eBird/Clements checklist of Birds of the World: v2025.
